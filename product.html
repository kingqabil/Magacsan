BOSWELLIA FREREANA RESIN Boswellia Frereana resin also known as the “King of Frankincense” , is a unique resin only found on the steep costal slopes and mountainous regions of Northern Somalia. Frereana resin also known as "Maydi" in Somali, is one of the oldest forms of chewing gum. Due to the little amount of water soluble gum present in the resin. Frereana resin, when chewed forms a chewing gum. The resin also can be used for incense, aromatherapy, and essential oil distillation. HOW TO USE Boswellia Frereana resin has many of the same uses as the Carterii resin. Frereana resin can be burned by simply placing a small amount of resin on an incense burner or lighting a charcoal disk for 1 to 3 minutes then placing the resin on top of the charcoal . HOW TO USE AS CHEWING GUM: Take small to medium size pea of the resin. Rinse it with water for a minute or until throughly cleaned. Pop it in your mouth start chewing while adding small amounts of water. You can add more resin to make the chewing gum more formed. Enjoy! DESCRIPTION: Colour ranges for Frereana resins can be opaque, semi- translucent white and light yellow to amber. Size:Assorted mix of very small to medium resin. SCENT: Frereana resin has a bolder scent then the Carterii resin. Earthy rich aroma, floral note with layers of spice.
